Education for All Initiative

Making quality learning accessible to everyone — regardless of background, language, or means.

Our mission

The Education for All Initiative was started in 2014 with a simple belief: knowledge is a right, not a privilege. The initiative was founded by Prof. Hasan Aljabbouli. Its goal is to lower the barriers that keep people from learning — cost, language, and access — and to put practical, high-quality education within reach of anyone who wants it.

What we do

  • 🌍
    Free and openCreate free, openly accessible learning resources and tools — released as open source so anyone can use, host, or build on them.
  • 🛠️
    Practical skillsFocus on practical skills that help people improve their work, their studies, and their livelihoods.
  • 🗣️
    Learners' own languageBuild multilingual by design — including Arabic and English, with right-to-left support — so no one is excluded by language.
  • 📶
    Built for low-resource settingsWork on modest infrastructure and without requiring an IT department, so access isn't limited to well-funded institutions.
  • 🔒
    Privacy and dignityProtect learner data by default — no surveillance, no lock-in, no selling of information.
  • 🤝
    Support educators, not replace themGive teachers tools that extend their reach, while they keep full control of their courses.

How LMS-Bridge fits in

LMS-Bridge is part of this initiative: a free, open-source tool that gives every student a patient, private guide at the exact moment they slip in a hard course. It turns the assessment data schools already collect into just-in-time, AI-guided tutoring that strengthens the foundational concepts learners need to move forward — guiding them to reason it out rather than handing over answers.

It runs inside any major learning platform, or standalone with nothing more than a join code, in multiple languages and without costly infrastructure — so it can reach students who would otherwise be left behind in large, under-resourced classrooms. It carries the initiative's goal forward: quality, personalized education, within reach of everyone.
