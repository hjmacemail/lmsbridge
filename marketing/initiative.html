Harnessing knowledge to empower people, solve real-world challenges, and create lasting social impact.

Our mission

The Knowledge in Service of Society Initiative was founded in 2014 by Prof. Hasan Aljabbouli with a simple belief: knowledge has its greatest value when it is shared, applied, and used to improve society.

Our mission is to make knowledge accessible, practical, and impactful by removing barriers such as cost, language, and limited access to quality educational resources. We believe that every individual should have the opportunity not only to learn but also to apply their knowledge to improve their lives, strengthen their communities, and contribute to solving local and global challenges.

How LMS-Bridge fits in this initiative

LMS-Bridge is a direct expression of this mission: it takes something normally scarce and expensive — a patient, one-on-one tutor — and makes it freely available to any student, in any course, regardless of their institution's budget. It turns the assessment data schools already collect into just-in-time, AI-guided tutoring that helps learners understand and apply foundational concepts, rather than simply handing over answers.

By removing the barriers of cost, language, and access — running inside any major learning platform or standalone, in multiple languages, without costly infrastructure — it puts practical knowledge to work improving real learning outcomes for the students who need it most. It is knowledge, shared and applied, in the service of the people it reaches.
